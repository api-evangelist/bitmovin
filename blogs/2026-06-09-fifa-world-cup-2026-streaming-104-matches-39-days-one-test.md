A goal in a World Cup knockout match is a near-instantaneous traffic event across tens of millions of concurrent sessions. In 2026, that happens across 104 matches, streamed across 212 territories, for a projected 6 billion total engagements; the largest live streaming test the industry has ever faced. Qatar 2022 proved streaming could handle global simultaneous demand.
